How to use VNC

Make sure that you have an instance that has a publicly accessible ip address. We will use ssh tunnelling to create a secure connection to your vnc server. You will also need a VNC client installed on your local machine that you will be using to connect to your instance.

  1. On your local machine, create an ssh tunnel:
     ssh -L 5901:127.0.0.1:5901 -C -N ubuntu@<remote-instance-ip>
  2. On your local machine, use your VNC client to connect to localhost:5901 . The password is nsg-dev .
